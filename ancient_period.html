Ancient Ethiopia

According to Ethiopians, the first inhabitants of Ethiopia were the family of Ham, the son of Noah. One of Ham's sons is Cush, who is the father of Ethiopia, known as Ethiopis. The Kushites who came from Mesopotamia also settled in the highlands of Ethiopia.
